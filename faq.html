Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VelvetZone

To create an account:

  1. Click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Choose whether you're signing up as a client or provider
  3. Fill in your details including email and password
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you
  5. Complete your profile setup

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

We take several measures to ensure user safety:

  • Identity Verification: All providers must complete identity verification
  • Blockchain Security: All communications are encrypted and secured with blockchain technology
  • Review System: Clients can rate and review providers after meetings
  • Reporting Tools: Easy-to-use reporting for any suspicious activity
  • Safety Tips: We provide comprehensive safety guidelines for all users

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Bank Transfers
  • Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT)

All transactions are secured with end-to-end encryption.

To become a verified provider:

  1. Sign up as a provider and complete your profile
  2. Submit your ID and additional verification documents
  3. Complete the verification interview (video call)
  4. Wait for our team to review your application (1-3 business days)
  5. Once approved, you'll receive a verified badge on your profile

Verification helps build trust with potential clients.
