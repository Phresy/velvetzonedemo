Safety Tips

Your security is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure safe experiences.

Identity Verification

  • Always verify your provider's identity through our platform
  • Look for the verified badge on profiles
  • Never share personal identification documents outside our secure system

Communication

  • Keep all communication on our platform until you establish trust
  • Be wary of anyone trying to move conversations to other apps immediately
  • Report any suspicious messages to our support team

Payments

  • Only use our secure payment system - never pay directly
  • Avoid providers who request payment through unofficial channels
  • All transactions are protected by blockchain technology

Meetups

  • Always meet in public places first
  • Inform a friend about your plans and share your location
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave

Red Flags

  • Requests for financial help or loans
  • Pressure to move communication off-platform quickly
  • Inconsistent information or stories
  • Refusal to verify identity through our system

Reporting

  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately
  • Use our emergency contact if you feel unsafe
  • Block users who make you uncomfortable
